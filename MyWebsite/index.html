My Personal Interests

I want my impact on the world to be beneficial. There are so many ways we impact this planet, either through the people we meet or products we consume. I do my best to recycle and use sustainable products that were manufactured minimally. I love fish and I want to help save our reefs.

Growing up I was never allowed to have pets, not even a fish. So now I have a fascination with them. I love to see their mannerisms. Right now I am figuring out how to sustain an aquarium that lets the fish thrive. The more happy I can make the creatures around me, the happier I feel.

I like working with electronics, they fascinate me. I currently have a job at Fixit Mobile where I repair phones, and laptops which always keeps me on my toes. In my free time I work on Arduino projects. It's a fun challenge that keeps me learning.
